Do you offer an discounts? Expand

Head over to our rates page, to see our multi track discount (upto 20%). The more work you need completing, the cheaper the price per track!

How long does mastering / mixing take to complete? Expand

We aim to complete all work within 7 working days, but this is dependant on workload. If your project is expected to take more than 7 days, a member of the team will get in touch with you.

If your seeking a quicker service, we offer priority mastering on all services. This incurrs a surcharge - check out the rates page for more detail.

Can I discuss my project with you before you begin working on the audio? Expand

Our main priority is getting the best out of our services for our customers, so understanding your requirements is a key part in acheiving this

Once you are ready to go ahead with any of our services, give us a call to discuss your needs.

How do I send you audio files and what format is required? Expand

Once an order is placed, we will send over a confirmation email with links to upload to either our Google Drive, or DropBox directories, as well as details on how to name your files and the formats we require files to be.

Do I pay before the work is started? Expand

Yes, all payments must be recieved in full to commission the work on your audio files

Can you make revisions once the work is complete? Expand

We want our customers to be happy with how their final product. That being said, if you feel that you'd like some changes to be made to the sound, then get in touch. We will work with you to ensure its how you imagined.
