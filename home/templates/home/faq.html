FAQ

How do I list a conversion on the site? Expand

To get a coversion of yours listed onto our site is simple!

  1. Firstly make sure you are signed in, or sign up if your new here.
  2. Then simply select 'Add a conversion' from the nav bar, or using the link in your profile.
  3. You will be presented with a form, which will gather all the information needed to get your conversion advertised. Make sure to have all this ready and some great photographs too!!
  4. Once this is completed, purchase a listing token and remember to assign your newly added listing to it on the product page!
  5. When your order is complete, within 24hours your listing will be live on the site. In the meantime, feel free to edit it as you please from 'my_listings' in your profile!

What happens to my listing after the listing token expires? Expand

Your listing will remain active in your account for a further 7days after expiry. If a new listing token isn't applied at the end of this period, the listing will be deleted from the server.
